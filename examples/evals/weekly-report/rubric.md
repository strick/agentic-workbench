# Rubric — Weekly Director Report

1. **Director-ready tone** — outcomes and asks, not a task log. No "todo",
   no bug-by-bug notes; the tone check enforces the obvious tells mechanically,
   a human judges the rest.
2. **Synthesis, not concatenation** — items from multiple days merge into
   themes (the APIM gateway thread should read as one storyline).
3. **Decisions and asks are explicit** — a director should know what was
   decided and what needs their weight (the Entra policy exception).
4. **Risks carry consequences** — each risk says what slips if ignored
   (Foundry capacity → pilot date).
5. **Traceable** — every claim traces to a source daily log.
